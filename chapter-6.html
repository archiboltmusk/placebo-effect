The Rebranding of Human Physiology

Traditional systems claim a monopoly on "holistic health" — the real innovation was rebranding basic physiology as a proprietary ancient secret.

Ch. 06

Traditional systems claim a monopoly on "holistic health" via diet, circadian rhythm, and lifestyle (Dinacharya). The proposal argues modern medicine always treated lifestyle as its primary metabolic defense — the real innovation was rebranding basic physiology as a proprietary ancient secret.

FrameworkLifestyle Medicine (Allopathy)Traditional Systems (Ayurveda)
Diagnostic basisVerifiable biomarkers, insulin sensitivity (GLUT4), vascular fluid dynamics.Metaphysical balancing of Doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha) and Agni.
ValidationRandomized double-blind trials; quantitative physiological tracking.Empirical reference to canonical texts (Charaka / Sushruta Samhita).
Commercial strategyLow-cost baseline defense (therapeutic lifestyle change).Packaged as exotic, high-margin premium wellness rituals and exports.
