APPX

Every empirical claim in this manuscript — drug-development cost ranges, statutory citations, toxicology findings, and clinical trial critiques — will be indexed here against its primary source, organized by the chapter in which it appears.

Planned Structure

  • Statutory & Regulatory Texts. Drugs and Cosmetics Act (1940) and amendments, AYUSH ministry notifications, and the specific sections governing P&P Ayurvedic licensing.
  • Clinical & Toxicology Literature. Peer-reviewed studies on heavy-metal content in Rasa Shastra formulations, drug-induced liver injury case series, and pharmacovigilance reports.
  • Market & Financial Data. Public filings, market-share estimates, and reported R&D expenditure for both allopathic and traditional-medicine manufacturers.
  • Case Documentation. Primary reporting and regulatory correspondence relating to incidents discussed in the text, such as the Coronil launch and subsequent review.
