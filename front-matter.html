Title Page & Framing Note

How this dossier was assembled, what counts as evidence inside it, and the line it refuses to cross before the case begins.

FM

The front matter establishes the terms of engagement before the investigation begins: the working title, a framing epigraph, and a short author's note on methodology — what counts as a source, what counts as evidence, and which claims in this manuscript are flagged for verification against primary literature prior to publication.

What Belongs Here

  • Title page. Working title, author, and the one-line positioning statement.
  • Epigraph. A short framing quotation, likely drawn from a regulatory text or a clinical case report.
  • A note on method. How claims are sourced, where this manuscript draws the line between author argument and verified citation, and how the appendix bibliography is structured.
