Cultural Nationalism as a Shield

When the scientific community raises concerns over molecular safety or heavy metals in Rasa Shastra formulations, the market activates its defense — and the defense is rhetorical, not clinical.

Ch. 05

When the scientific community raises concerns over molecular safety, diagnostic replication, or heavy metals (lead, mercury, arsenic) in Rasa Shastra formulations, the market activates its defense.

  • The Identity Pivot. Debates over pharmacovigilance converted into emotional battles over national heritage and decolonization.
  • The Anti-Science Rhetoric. Anyone demanding falsifiable evidence labeled Eurocentric or bio-colonialist — insulating the economy from skepticism.
